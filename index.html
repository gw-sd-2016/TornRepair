GW CS Senior Design 2016

Tan Wang: Torn Repair

This project pieces the torn photo parts or torn document parts together, and generates a result photo or document. It also fills the missing pixel along the edges, detects image parts from multiple original photo, and generates an album for the multipage case. The fragments can be either torn (jagged edge) or shred ( straight edge)

Bio: Hi, my name is Tan Wang. I am a currently a college student major in Computer Science in George Washington University. I also have two minors, one is in Economics and the other one is in Business Administration. I will start my Master of Science study in Computer Science in the same university right after I graduate.
